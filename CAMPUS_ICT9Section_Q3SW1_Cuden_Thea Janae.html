The Legends Of The Blue Sea

This drama is based on a traditional Joseon legend about a fisherman who captures and kidnaps a mermaid. It chronicles the love tale of Kim Dam Ryung and Se Hwa, a mermaid he named Se Hwa. Heo Joon Jae is now a smooth-talking conman with magician-like talents. He poses as a prosecutor and defrauds a wealthy suspect's mother before fleeing to Spain and staying at an oceanfront resort. Shim Chung is a mermaid who is trapped in a tidal wave and washes ashore near the resort, where she meets Joon Jae.

CHARACTERS

    Kal So-won as child Se-hwa
    Shin Eun-soo as teenage Se-hwa
    Lee Min-ho as Heo Joon-jae / Kim Dam-ryeong
    Jeon Jin-seo [ko] as child Heo Joon-jae / Kim Dam-ryeong
    Park Jin-young as teenage Heo Joon-jae / Kim Dam-ryeong
